Clear Coat

Clear car paint not only makes your car look shiny and more glamorous, it makes the car paint look wetter and deeper. However, there are other functional advantages that are of greater importance. 


One major advantage is that this coat provides protection from the sun and ultraviolet rays that cause the car paint to fade. Hence, your car paint appears new for a longer period of time

Hardener

Activator is an interchangeable name for catalyst or hardener. The activator helps the basecoat or clear coat cure and harden which prevents bubbling or fading of the paint.

Primer

Primers are the most common undercoats used when refinishing car bodies, Industrial Applications and Signs. They’re used to build up and level feathered areas or rough surfaces and to provide a smooth surface for paint.
