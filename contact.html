International

International

Mipa

MIPA SE, headquartered in Essenbach/Germany, is a fast-growing medium-sized company producing varnishes and paints. There are seven further plants at home and abroad where we are developing and producing automotive refinishing paints, industrial and decorative paints as well as wood paints for professional users.


More than 1400 employees are working all over the world for Mipa Group which is currently sending products to its subsidiaries and customers in more than 100 countries on all continents.


MIPA bears the subtitle Professional Coating Systems. According to this leitmotif, they develop, produce and sell coatings with a focus on lacquers and paints. We have been MIPA importers for decades, our aim is to deliver the highest quality paint solution to our customers.

Smirdex

Smirdex creates high-tech and top-quality abrasive materials and relative accessories, meeting the evolving and demanding needs of a vast variety of professionals. We constantly invest in research and development so as to ensure that our products will always be a step ahead of the competition.

Focusing its commercial activity on the international market, Smirdex trades 85% of its products in more than 72 countries worldwide, through its network of dealers and associates.

Tuba

In just a few years, TUBA has built its reputation in the coating industry with its quality products.

Combined with its engineering-driven culture, rich industrial experiences and manufacturing know-how, TUBA is the only manufacturer in the world that can provide a full line of hi-end spray guns with totally no rubber o-ring design for extended durability.

In the automotive refinishing market, TUBA equipment provides excellent atomization of high-solids clears that leads to glass-like finishes. TUBA equipment is optimized to virtually eliminate mottling and striping with metallic base coats and produce an excellent colour match.

Miarco

The company is characterized by its own corporate philosophy in which customer orientation and ethics, together with a strong team spirit and a focus on results, are fundamental pieces to offer its customers excellent, fully tailored adhesive solutions for sectors as diverse as: paper mills, printing presses, graphic arts, the automotive industry, fairs and events, pharmaceuticals, packaging, food and beverages, painting and hardware, DIY, or construction, among others.
